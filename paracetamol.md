# Paracetamol 500mg

## Description

Paracetamol is a commonly used analgesic and antipyretic medication.

## Indications

- Mild to moderate pain
- Headache
- Toothache
- Fever
- Muscle aches
- Cold and flu symptoms

## Dosage

Adults: 500mg to 1000mg every 4 to 6 hours.
Maximum: 4000mg per day.

## Contraindications

- Severe liver disease
- Hypersensitivity to paracetamol

## Side Effects

- Nausea
- Allergic reactions (rare)
